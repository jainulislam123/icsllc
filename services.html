Interconnection Services

If you are a carrier or service provider, our interconnection services provides long distance and international network access for your voice traffic. Aggressive rates across the world, with focus on strong destinations such as South Asia, Middle East, Africa, Europe & Latin America.

E1 and IP Access

ICS LLC provides bearer access utilizing either E1's or IP. This is a direct, uncontended connection from your equipment to the switch. This access product can also be used to connect your customers to your own infrastructure and is available between connected buildings.

CTS (Call Termination Service)

The most popular among our Voice product. CTS is the termination of minutes across the VoIP Network. We route the call to the far end for handoff to the destination carrier and termination to their user. Offering competitive rates and high call quality, CTS is available on E1 or IP access products.

Carrier Interconnect

We perform third party interconnection services. This service includes starting from business case study to interconnection test for ultimate production for both Carrier and Suppliers. This is best achieved in a customer-supplier data center with a large ecosystem of collocation together.
